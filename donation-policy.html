Donation Policy

Ek Paryas Foundation

All donations made to Ek Paryas Foundation are voluntary and are used to support our charitable activities and social initiatives.

Use of Donations

Donations received are utilized for education support, women empowerment, animal care, community development, and related operational expenses.

Refund Policy

Donations are generally non-refundable. Any genuine concerns may be addressed on a case-by-case basis.

Tax Exemption

Tax exemption benefits (such as 80G) may not be available at present. Donors are requested to donate accordingly.

Transparency

We are committed to transparency and responsible use of donated funds.
